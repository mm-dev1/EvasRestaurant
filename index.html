Since June 15, 1989, our restaurant has been a cherished family endeavor. Originally purchased by our sister, María Robles, it was later acquired by Eduardo and Irene Zúñiga and Rubén and Verónica Guzmán on December 18, 1998. Our dream was to build a family business where we could work together harmoniously and create a brighter future for our children.

Today, we are proud to see our family thrive: Cynthia and Héctor Zúñiga are a Medical Doctor and Criminology graduate, respectively, while Rubén Guzmán specializes as a Emergency Physician, and Giselle Guzmán holds dual degrees in Psychology and Criminology.

We serve American breakfasts, authentic Mexican dishes, and fresh seafood, all prepared with dedication and care. Our goal is to leave a lasting legacy in the community, creating beautiful memories for families who gather at our restaurant.

Thank You

Feb 2025

Honored by Our Community

For decades, Eva’s Restaurant was more than just a place to eat—it was a cherished part of Bell Gardens. We were honored when the Bell Gardens City Council, Congressman Robert Garcia, Senator Lena Gonzalez, and Assemblywoman Blanca Pacheco awarded us certificates of recognition for our authentic, high-quality Mexican food. This recognition reflects the love and dedication we poured into every dish and moment shared with customers. We are truly grateful for the support we’ve received throughout the years—your kindness made Eva’s what it was. While our doors have closed, our memories and the legacy of Eva’s will live on forever in the hearts of many.

Feb 2025

Heartfelt Thank You

Receiving these awards was not just about food but a celebration of the connections we built with our community. Being one of the few long-standing family-owned businesses in Bell Gardens was an honor, and we owe it all to you—our customers, neighbors, and friends.
While Eva’s Restaurant has now closed, the love and memories we shared will remain forever. Your support, loyalty, and friendship made this journey unforgettable. We are beyond thankful for every meal, every conversation, and every moment spent together. Though our restaurant is gone, the warmth and spirit of Eva’s will always be part of the community’s heart.
